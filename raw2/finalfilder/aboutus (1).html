Mission Statement

The purpose of this business startup is to make indigenous and trivial services across cities and towns available to all citizens through just a touch on their smartphones. It will also be a great oppurtunity for the locals in that area to expand their services through their quality of work.

Vision Statement

This idea envisions to encompass all the local businesses and service providers into one conglomerated business centre making it feasible for common people in that area.

Target Market Summary

We aim to pay heed to the parochial services like manpower for construction and construction contracts , local transportation services like booking autos, cabs etc to household repairs like electricians , plumbers etc .This idea can be further expanded to cover local goods , vegetables , groceries and so on. Overall this proposition adheres to making parish facilities easily accessible for the natives and expanding native business ideas by increasing their reach to public.
